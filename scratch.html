Fun Facts about my Life in China!

  1. I spent 18 years living in Shanghai!.
    • Fun Fact: I am American.
    • I moved to America for college when I was 18 years old.
  2. I went to Chinese local school in first grade.
    • I was the only blonde and white kid in my entire school.
    • I would ask my parents to dye my hair black.
  3. People would touch my pale skin and hair because it was lucky.
    • I joked with people charging them 100 RMB to take a picture.
    • People offer me gifts because they thought it was lucky.
    • Locals keep photo albums of foreigners for luck.
